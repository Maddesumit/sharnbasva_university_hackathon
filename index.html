About #EnlightenCode_2.0

#EnlightenCode_2.0 is an intercollege hackathon that unites innovative minds to forge tomorrow's solutions today. Hosted by Sharnbasava University, we bring together passionate student developers, designers, and problem-solvers in an immersive hackathon experience.

Top 3 teams will win exciting cash prizes and top 10 teams will receive gift hampers!

About Sharnbasava University

Sharnbasava University, established with a vision to provide quality education, is committed to technological excellence, innovative learning, and holistic education. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, strong placement records, and impactful industry connections, we develop transformative engineers ready to address global challenges through cutting-edge knowledge and practical expertise.

Who Can Apply?

#EnlightenCode_2.0 welcomes all college students from across India, irrespective of the degree they are pursuing and for undergraduate (UG) students only. The team can be of interdisciplinary & Intercollege participants.

Team Formation

A team must have minimum of 3 and maximium 5 members, all from the same or different institution.

Guidelines

"#EnlightenCode_2.0 Innovate Early, Shine Bright"

  • Team Size: 3–5 members. members can be from inter-institutional also teams allowed.
  • Transportation and accommodation: Must be arranged by the participants.food, snacks, tea/coffee, and nap area will be provided.
  • Identity Proof: Required for verification during registration and entry.
  • Registration Fee: ₹100 per student, paid via Google Form at this link.
  • Resources: Teams are responsible for bringing their own hardware and datasets.
  • In-person Attendance: Mandatory throughout the event.
  • Pre-Built Projects: Participants must bring fully functional pre-built products along with a presentation for Round 1. No problem statements will be provided.
  • Certificates: Every participant will receive a participation certificate.
  • Domain Change: Once registered, teams cannot change their project domain.
  • Event Themes: AI & Machine Learning, Web3 & Blockchain, Sustainability, Healthcare, Agriculture, Banking.
  • Evaluation Criteria: Idea novelty, complexity, clarity, feasibility, practicability, and scalability.
  • Rule Modifications: Organizers reserve the right to update contest dates and rules.
  • Code of Conduct: Follow inclusive behavior. Cheating or misconduct leads to disqualification. Maintain harassment-free environment. Participants must remain on-site during the event.

Evaluation Schedule

  • Round 1 - Presentation Round: 9:00 AM onwards. All teams present their pre-built solution.
  • Round 2 - Monitor and Update: Post lunch. All teams receive mentoring and refine their solution.
  • Round 3 - Final Showdown: Shortlisted top 10 teams Receive Cash Prizes and Gift Hampers.

Prizes

  • Top 3 Teams: Exciting cash prizes.
  • Top 10 Teams: Gift hampers for all team members.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is #EnlightenCode_2.0?

#EnlightenCode_2.0 is an intercollege hackathon hosted at Sharnbasava University, Kalaburagi. It's a 12-hour challenge where students from across the country come together to showcase their pre-built projects and complete tasks provided by judges.

Who can participate?

#EnlightenCode_2.0 welcomes all college students from across India, irrespective of the degree they are pursuing. Whether you're an undergraduate student, a seasoned programmer, a creative designer, or simply someone enthusiastic about technology and innovation, this hackathon is for you!

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a registration fee of 100 per member

How do I register?

You can register by clicking the "Register Now" button on this page. Complete the registration form, and you'll receive a confirmation email with further details to submit your project details.

Will food and accommodation be provided?

Yes! We'll provide meals, snacks, and refreshments throughout the event. Accommodation will not be provided
