About HackSBU

HackSBU is a premier campus hackathon that unites innovative minds to forge tomorrow's solutions today. As Sharnbasava University's flagship event, we bring together passionate student developers, designers, and problem-solvers in an immersive hackathon experience.

With a prize pool of ₹10 thousand and a chance to solve real-world problems, HackSBU is your platform to showcase your skills and WIN BIG!

About Sharnbasava University

Sharnbasava University, established with a vision to provide quality education, is committed to technological excellence, innovative learning, and holistic education. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, strong placement records, and impactful industry connections, we develop transformative engineers ready to address global challenges through cutting-edge knowledge and practical expertise.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HackSBU?

HackSBU is a nationwide hackathon hosted at Sharnbasava University, Kalaburagi. It's a 12-hour challenge where students from across the country come together to create impactful solutions to real-world problems.

Who can participate?

HackSBU welcomes all college students from across India, irrespective of the degree they are pursuing. Whether you're an undergraduate student, a seasoned programmer, a creative designer, or simply someone enthusiastic about technology and innovation, this hackathon is for you!

Is there a registration fee?

No, there is no upfront registration or participation fee for HackSBU. However, once your team is shortlisted, a non-refundable security fee of ₹500 per team will be required to confirm your participation.

How do I register?

You can register by clicking the "Register Now" button on this page. Complete the registration form, and you'll receive a confirmation email with further details to submit your idea.

Will food and accommodation be provided?

Yes! We'll provide meals, snacks, and refreshments throughout the event. Accommodation for outstation participants will also be arranged on prior request.
